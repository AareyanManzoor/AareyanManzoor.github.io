LaTeX workshop

The aim of this workshop is to teach LaTeX by using it. Here are the parameters:

  • Every participant will type up approximately a page or 2 of some paper chosen for the particular workshop.
  • By the end, you will know all the basics of latex: typesetting, writing commands, different fonts, good conventions etc.
  • You can also put your name on the typeset paper, which will be a part of the TeXromancers

    • Here are some resources:

  • Detexify, a tool that will help you identify what the LaTeX code corresponding to a symbol.
  • List of LaTeX symbols
  • quiver, to make commutative diagrams.
